The voice.py file contains all the phrases your Pwnagotchi uses to communicate.
You can replace the original voice.py with my custom version to give your device a more unique and expressive personality.
Feel free to look inside first and edit! It is a simple Format!

Connect to your Pwnagotchi
Use FileZilla (or any SFTP client) to access your device.

Navigate to the voice file

/home/pi/.pwn/lib/python3.11/site-packages/pwnagotchi/voice.py

Replace the file

Restart your Pwnagotchi

You may need root access to modify system files.
If FileZilla gives permission errors, try connecting as pi and enabling SFTP or SSH with elevated rights.
